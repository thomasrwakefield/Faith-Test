Ministries

Ministries & Gatherings for Every Generation

Faith Church is full of environments designed to help you belong, grow, and join God's mission in Springfield and beyond.

Weekend Gatherings

Two identical services every Sunday at 9:00 and 11:00 AM blend passionate worship, biblical teaching, communion, and prayer ministry.

  • Dynamic worship led by our multi-ethnic team
  • Age-specific environments for kids and students
  • Live Spanish translation and ASL interpretation
  • Prayer partners available after every service

Kids & Students

We champion the next generation through engaging lessons, discipleship, and meaningful relationships.

  • Faith Kids (Birth – Grade 5) with creative Bible curriculum
  • Faith Students (Grades 6-12) weekly gatherings and small groups
  • Student leadership teams and annual camps
  • Safety-certified volunteers and secure check-in

Groups & Classes

Discipleship happens best in circles, not rows. Find a group that fits your schedule and stage of life.

  • Home groups in neighborhoods across Springfield
  • Men's and women's studies focused on Scripture
  • Financial Peace, Alpha, and Parenting on Purpose courses
  • Spiritual formation retreats each spring and fall

Care & Counseling

Our pastoral team and trained volunteers offer confidential support in seasons of crisis, grief, and transition.

  • Prayer appointments and prophetic ministry rooms
  • Support groups for grief, addiction recovery, and caregivers
  • Marriage mentoring and premarital coaching
  • Referrals to trusted professional counselors

Local & Global Outreach

We partner with schools, nonprofits, and missionaries to demonstrate the compassion of Jesus.

  • Monthly food pantry and community meal teams
  • Mentoring partnerships with Jefferson Elementary
  • Short-term mission trips to Central America and Southeast Asia
  • Financial support for 18 global workers and church plants

Discover Your Best Next Step

New to Faith Church? Join the Discover Track, a four-week journey that shares our story, highlights your spiritual gifts, and helps you find a ministry team or group where you can thrive.

Sessions begin on the first Sunday of every month at 11:00 AM. Childcare is provided and lunch is included.
