Discover Track Schedule

The Discover Track meets in the Community Room on the first floor. You will enjoy lunch with staff, hear our story, and identify your spiritual gifts. Kids enjoy their own fun experience.

  • Week 1: Know God — Discover the gospel, worship, and prayer.
  • Week 2: Find Freedom — Learn how groups and pastoral care support your journey.
  • Week 3: Discover Purpose — Identify gifts, passions, and personality.
  • Week 4: Make a Difference — Join a serve team and live sent.

Baptism Sundays

We celebrate baptisms quarterly during our weekend gatherings. Participants attend a brief class and share a personal testimony of how Jesus has changed their life.

  • Preparation class held the Wednesday before baptism
  • Professional photos and video testimony provided
  • Family seating and celebration brunch included
  • Kids and students participate with parental guidance

Membership Pathway

Membership at Faith Church means partnering with a spiritual family committed to Jesus, each other, and Springfield. After completing Discover Track, you will meet with a pastor, review our Statement of Faith, and share your story.

Members commit to a shared rule of life: practicing spiritual disciplines, gathering consistently, giving generously, serving faithfully, and living on mission beyond the walls of the church.

We host a membership celebration twice a year to pray for new members and commission them to serve our city.
