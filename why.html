About Faith Church

Why Faith Church Exists in Springfield

We are a multi-generational church compelled by the gospel to cultivate hope, healing, and Holy Spirit transformation in every neighborhood of our city.

Anchored in the Good News of Jesus

Faith Church began with a simple conviction: when people encounter Jesus and live out the Scriptures in everyday life, entire communities are renewed. Every ministry, gathering, and outreach we launch flows from this conviction. We teach the Bible with clarity, celebrate the sacraments, and create spaces where people can experience the presence of God.

Our weekend services weave together heartfelt worship, prayer, and teaching that connects the timeless truth of Scripture to real-life challenges. We embrace the Holy Spirit's work in our midst and we regularly celebrate stories of healing, deliverance, and transformation. These rhythms fuel our commitment to share the hope of Jesus across Springfield.

Guiding Values That Shape Our Culture

  • Christ-Centered: Jesus is the head of the Church and the focus of every sermon, song, and strategy.
  • Relational Discipleship: We invest in people through mentoring, groups, and intentional pathways that help believers mature.
  • Kingdom Compassion: We respond to injustice and suffering with tangible acts of service fueled by prayer.
  • Generational Unity: From kids to seniors, every age is valued, empowered, and released to minister.
  • Spirit-Led Boldness: We listen for God's voice and step into new ventures with faith and courage.

Five Pillars of Our Calling

In prayerful collaboration with our elders and ministry leaders, we identified five pillars that keep us aligned with our mission. These pillars inform the way we budget, hire, serve, and evaluate impact.

  1. Weekend Encounter: Creating worship gatherings where people meet Jesus and respond to the gospel.
  2. Everyday Formation: Offering classes, groups, and coaching that equip followers of Jesus to live on mission in their homes and workplaces.
  3. Next Generation Investment: Raising resilient kids, students, and young adults who know their identity and purpose in Christ.
  4. Compassionate Outreach: Partnering with local agencies and global missionaries to meet spiritual and practical needs.
  5. Leadership Development: Training emerging leaders who will plant churches, launch ministries, and influence culture.

Impact Across Springfield

Faith Church is strategically located in the heart of Springfield, giving us front-row seats to the challenges and opportunities facing our neighbors. We host monthly food distributions, mentor students in local schools, and collaborate with civic leaders to promote peace and unity. Our building is open throughout the week as a house of prayer, a resource hub, and a safe environment for community partnerships.

The transformation we long for is bigger than a Sunday service. It is the steady, faithful presence of believers living out the gospel on their streets, in their workplaces, and in their families. Our role as a church is to equip and encourage that everyday mission.

How You Can Be Part of the Story

Whether you are exploring faith, returning to church after time away, or searching for a place where your gifts can make a difference, Faith Church welcomes you. Start by joining us for a weekend gathering, then take a next step through one of our connection environments. Together, we will continue to build a church that helps people know Jesus, grow in freedom, and live sent.
