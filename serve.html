Serve

Serve Springfield with Faith Church

You were created to make a difference. From Sunday teams to global missions, there is a place where your gifts and passion can flourish.

Sunday Experience Teams

Create a warm welcome each weekend through hospitality, worship, production, and prayer ministry.

  • Guest Services, ushers, and parking teams
  • Worship musicians, choir, and vocalists
  • Production team (audio, video, lighting)
  • Prayer and prophetic ministry partners

Next Generation Teams

Invest in kids, students, and young adults as they discover their identity and purpose in Christ.

  • Nursery, preschool, and elementary leaders
  • Student small group mentors and event support
  • College ministry coaches and internship mentors
  • Background checks and training provided for every volunteer

Community Compassion Teams

Bring hope to Springfield through ongoing partnerships with schools, shelters, and local nonprofits.

  • Monthly food pantry, clothing closet, and meal prep
  • School mentorship and classroom adoption
  • Disaster relief and crisis response teams
  • Immigrant and refugee support through ESL tutoring

How to Join a Serve Team

  1. Step 1: Attend Discover Track. Learn about our culture, leadership, and opportunities to serve.
  2. Step 2: Meet a Team Coach. We will help you identify gifts, passions, and schedule availability.
  3. Step 3: Shadow & Train. Spend a few weeks alongside a mentor learning best practices.
  4. Step 4: Serve with Joy. Commit to a rotation that fits your season and celebrate stories of life change.

We provide ongoing coaching, leadership development, and appreciation gatherings for every volunteer. Your yes matters!
