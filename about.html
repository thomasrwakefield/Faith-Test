About Us

Who We Are

Faith Church is a family of believers from diverse backgrounds learning to follow Jesus together.

We exist to create a welcoming, hope-filled environment where people can encounter God, discover purpose, and build meaningful relationships.

Our History

Faith Church began in a small living room in 1968 and has steadily grown into a vibrant multi-generational congregation committed to serving Springfield.

Through decades of change, our mission has remained the same: to help people know Jesus, grow in community, and live sent into the world.
